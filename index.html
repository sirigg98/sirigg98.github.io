Sirig Gurung

Hello! My name is Sirig and I am from Kathmandu, Nepal. I currently work as a predoctoral research fellow at Stanford Institute for Economic Policy and Research (SIEPR). I work Melanie Morten and Jeremy Weinstein on large-scale RCTs that encourage temporary migration in Mexico and Niger. My primary interests lie in Development, Migration and Networks.

Previously, I was lucky to work as a research assistant to Davin Chor at the Tuck School of Business, conducting research on Trade and the Political Economy. I graduated from Amherst College in 2021 with a B.A. in Economics and Mathematics.
