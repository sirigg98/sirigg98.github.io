    with Andrew Bernard, Esther BÃ¸ler, Davin Chor and Wei Lu

    Has large-scale censorship in China affected the diffusion on knowledge in economics? We study changes in citations for papers by faculty who host research on google sites, which was banned in China in 2010 with the introduction of the Great Firewall (GFC). Data was collected by scraping Google Scholar and faculty personal websites. As hypothesized, our results suggest that there were strong, persistent effects of the policy on citations for papers with at least one author with a google site - stay tuned for draft!
